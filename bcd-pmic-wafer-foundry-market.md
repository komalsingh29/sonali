<H2>BCD (PMIC) Wafer Foundry Market Overview </H2>
BCD (Bipolar-CMOS-DMOS) wafer foundry refers to a semiconductor manufacturing facility that specializes in producing wafers using BCD technology, which integrates Bipolar, CMOS, and DMOS processes on a single chip. This technology is widely used for Power Management ICs (PMICs), enabling high-efficiency power conversion, signal processing, and power control in various electronic applications.
