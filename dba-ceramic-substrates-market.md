DBA Ceramic Substrates Market Overview
The Direct Bonded Aluminum (DBA) Ceramic Substrates are advanced materials widely used in high-performance power electronics due to their excellent thermal conductivity, electrical insulation, and mechanical strength. They are particularly well-suited for high-voltage and high-temperature applications in electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, industrial inverters, and rail traction.
