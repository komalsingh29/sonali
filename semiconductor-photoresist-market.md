Semiconductor Photoresist Market Overview
Photoresist (PR) is a photosensitive chemical material essential to photolithography, a core technology in semiconductor manufacturing. When exposed to light, it initiates a chemical reaction that enables precise pattern transfer from a photomask to a silicon wafer, allowing for the creation of smaller, power-efficient, and high-performance circuits.
